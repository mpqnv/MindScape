Rhythmic Breath

Inhale as the circle expands. Exhale as it shrinks. Do this for 2 minutes to lower your heart rate.

Sensory Sync

Focus on the weight of your feet on the floor. Listen for the farthest sound you can hear. Bring your mind back to the present.

Mental De-clutter

Imagine your thoughts are browser tabs. Close the ones you aren't using right now. Focus only on the current "active window."
